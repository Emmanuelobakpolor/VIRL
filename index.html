Frequently Asked Questions

Got answers to common questions about our training programs and services

1 What training formats do you offer?

We offer in-person training and customized on-site corporate training designed to maximize learning outcomes based on your specific needs and preferences.

2 Do you provide certification upon completion?

Yes, we provide certification from VANESS upon successful completion of our training programs. As Center for Management Development(CMD) accredited and certified consultants, many of our programs also prepare you for industry-recognized certifications.

3 What is your refund and cancellation policy?

We offer a flexible cancellation policy. Full refunds are available up to 14 days before the training start date. Cancellation within 7-14 days recieve a 50% refund, and those within 7 days receive a credit for future training programs.

4 What Materials and resources are included?

All training programs include comprehensive course materials, digital resources template and case studies.
